A Clock

This is an animated clock. Around the face is a Latin phrase, 'Tempus fugit ut umbra, hic in spiratu, priusquam capias evanuit.', which means 'Time flits like a shadow, here in a breath, gone before you grasp it.' It is followed by another phrase, 'Tempus donum est; electio ars est', which means 'Time is a gift; choice is an art.' The two phrases are separated by a pair of crescent moon bullets. The face of the clock is a silver and gold yin/yang symbol, representing day/night duality.
